To allow new staff members to securely submit all required personal and emergency contact information, and then place their account into a 'Pending' status until an Admin officially reviews and activates their access.

üîë Key Components (What It Does)

    1. Extended Data Collection: Instead of just getting an email and password, the system now securely collects all necessary HR/compliance details upfront, including Residential Address and Two Next of Kin contacts.

    2. Non-Robot Verification (CAPTCHA): A simple challenge is included on the registration form to confirm the user is a human and prevent automated sign-ups (bots).

    3. Pending Account Creation: The user's information is saved, and their password is encrypted, but their account is marked as 'Pending'. They cannot log in yet.

    4. Admin Notification & OTP: A notification (like a "pop-up" or alert) is sent to the designated management/HR staff. This notification includes a One-Time Password (OTP) that only the manager can use.

    5. Admin Approval: The manager logs in, reviews the new staff member's complete details, and uses the special OTP to change the user's status from 'Pending' to 'Active'.

    6. Role/Permission Assignment: Once approved, the Admin immediately assigns the correct Role (e.g., "Standard Staff," "Manager," etc.) and Permissions to control what the staff member can see and do in the system.

The feature includes security measures to protect both the system and the sensitive user data:

    Data Protection: All submitted data is immediately stored securely in your database, and passwords are hashed (made irreversible).

    Controlled Access: The primary security measure is that no new user can access the system until a trusted Admin manually grants them permission and a specific role. This prevents unauthorized staff from gaining access simply by signing up.

In short, it's a secure, gated application process that ensures only verified staff members are onboarded, with all necessary data collected and approved by management.
