## üì¨ Understanding Conversations

## ‚úâÔ∏è Sending Messages

### How to Reply:
1. **Click into a conversation** to view the message thread
2. **Type your message** in the text box at the bottom
3. **Choose message type:**
   - **Public** (default) - Customer will see this
   - **Private** - Only your team sees this (notes/internal comments)
4. **Send message:**
   - Click the **Send button** OR
   - Press **Ctrl + Enter** (keyboard shortcut)
5. **Your message appears instantly** in the thread

### Message Tips:
- Messages are limited to 1,000 characters
- Character counter shows at the bottom right
- If the connection drops, you'll see a "Reconnecting..." message
- Messages are still sent even if reconnecting - they'll sync automatically

### Message Privacy:
- **Public messages** ‚Üí Customer sees these in their chat
- **Private messages** ‚Üí Only your team sees these (internal notes, reminders)
- Toggle the switch before sending to pick the right type

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I edit or delete a message?
**A:** No, messages can't be edited or deleted once sent. Always double-check before clicking Send!

### Q: What if a customer replies after I mark it Resolved?
**A:** No problem! The conversation automatically reopens. You'll get a notification.

### Q: Do other team members see my private messages?
**A:** Yes, all team members see private messages. They're just hidden from customers.

### Q: What happens if I lose internet connection?
**A:** The app shows "Reconnecting..." and keeps trying. Once reconnected, all updates sync automatically. Your drafted message stays in the box.

### Q: Can I see old messages?
**A:** Yes! Scroll up in the message thread to see the full conversation history.

### Q: Why didn't I get a notification?
**A:** Make sure you:
1. Clicked "Allow" when asked for notification permission
2. Didn't close the browser tab
3. Have the browser tab in focus (or browser window active)

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### **Problem: Messages not appearing**
- Refresh the page (F5)
- Check if you're connected (look for "Reconnecting..." message)
- Try closing and reopening the conversation

### **Problem: No notifications arriving**
- Check browser notification settings
- Make sure you allowed notifications when first using the app
- Some browsers disable notifications for background tabs

### **Problem: Can't send message**
- Check that message isn't empty
- Look at the character count (max 1,000)
- Verify connection is active (no "Reconnecting" message)

### **Problem: Typing indicator shows but no message appears**
- They might have closed the app or lost connection
- Just wait - they'll likely send the message when reconnected
- No need to prompt them again
